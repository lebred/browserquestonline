🧭 Progression 1 → 80 (v0.22.0)

The game runs on 5-floor tiers. Every 5th floor is a checkpoint floor with teleporter return access. The practical “level 60 journey” lives across floors 1-75, with floors 76-80 acting as a final stretch tier.

Tier and Checkpoint Rules

  • Tier formula: tier = floor((max(1,floor)-1)/5)
  • Recommended level: 1 + 4*tier
  • Checkpoint floors: 5,10,15,...,80
  • Teleporter unlock: first unlock happens at floor 5, then every +5 reached.
  • Rough level-60 destination: floor band 71-75 (tier 14, recommended level 57), with 76-80 as overcap/chase content.

Teleporters and Floor Entry

Teleport choices are generated from unlocked checkpoints only. If max floor is 57, available teleport floors are 5/10/15/20/25/30/35/40/45/50/55.

Checkpoint floors include a town return tile and still keep danger pockets away from stairs/portal to prevent trivial spawn loops.

Level 60 Path Notes

  • The smoothest route to level 60 is not pure floor-rushing. Expect to cycle farm loops around 35-39, 44-49, 54-59, and finally 71-75.
  • Enemy move speed now scales by +2% per floor, so deep-floor routing gets meaningfully more dangerous even before raw damage is considered.
  • Stronger enemies have better drop odds and better rarity odds, so targeted elite farming becomes more valuable in the late game.
  • If progression stalls, improve boots + ring quality first; those two slots often decide whether you can survive deep-floor pressure long enough to upgrade the rest.
