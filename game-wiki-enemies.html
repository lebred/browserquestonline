👾 Enemies and Drop Bands (v0.21.8)

Enemies scale by floor and tier, then branch by elite roll. Starting at floor 11, environmental lava adds sustained pressure in addition to combat damage. Archons and dragons are ranged casters, and elite enemies also have better loot odds than baseline mobs.

Spawn and Respawn Notes

  • Target enemy density increases with floor and tier, then capped for performance.
  • Respawn interval gets shorter when floor density drops (anti-empty-floor behavior).
  • Checkpoint floors (`5/10/15/...`) include teleporter return tile and still maintain combat pressure away from specials.
Warning: Floor 41+ is tuned as endgame. If your upgrades are below late astral/ancients quality, farm 31-40 before pushing.
